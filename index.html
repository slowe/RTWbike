On Tuesday April 22, 1884 Thomas Stevens set off from San Francisco and cycled around the world on a Penny Farthing. On Tuesday April 22, 2014 I started to recreate his journey* across the US raising money for MSF (Doctors Without Borders). I updated my progress via tweets, blog, photos and an updating map (thanks Arfon!). You can now see my US elevation profile. In 2015 I recreated the leg from Liverpool to Istanbul.

May 2 to June 29, 2015

* The route and timings but I'm not doing it on a Penny Farthing.
